Marina Barrage

Located at the mouth of the Singapore River,

The barrage not only serves its functional purpose of a dam, preventing floods and collecting freshwater, but also a popular recreational area! A magnificent piece of infrastructure overlooking the scenic view of Singapore skyline, the barrage offers many facilities, brining together friends, families and even communities.


Roof Garden

With the size of 4 football fields, the green pasture allows for an array of activities, from having picnics, flying kites, mass communal exercise, and even an ideal spot to watch the national day parade from!

Water Playground

Escape the heat get into the water! From the Water Jet Tunnel, Bubbling Play, and Fountain Square, there ain't a better way to cool off and have fun at the same time, just don't forget to pack an extra set of clothes!

Marina Bridge

With viewing pods that look out over the sea horizon, visitors can take a breather in the pods and enjoy the breeze. The bridge also connects the barrage to the Marina East Park, a popular route for joggers and cyclists.

Singapore River

Fancy kayaking with the view of the Singapore skyline? Enjoy other water sports in this naturally created reservoir.
